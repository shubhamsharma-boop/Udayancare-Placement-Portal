Who We Are

Udayan Care Placement Portal

The Udayan Care Placement Portal connects talented youth with trusted employers. It supports candidates in building meaningful careers while enabling employers to access skilled and job-ready talent.

Our objective is to bridge the gap between employability and employment through technology, mentorship and strong industry partnerships.

Vision

Empowering young people through sustainable livelihood opportunities and career development.

Mission

To connect deserving candidates with responsible employers through a transparent, technology-enabled placement ecosystem.
