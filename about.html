WHO WE ARE

Udayan Care Placement Portal

Udayan Care Placement Portal (UCPP) is a technology-enabled employment platform connecting Shalini Fellows with verified employers across India.

The portal helps candidates create their digital profile, explore career opportunities, apply for jobs and track their employment journey.

For employers, UCPP provides access to skilled, trained and job-ready talent through a transparent recruitment ecosystem.
