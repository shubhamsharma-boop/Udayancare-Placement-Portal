FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Need Answers?

Here are some of the most common questions asked by candidates and employers.

Can anyone register on the portal?

The portal is designed primarily for Udayan Care Shalini Fellows. Registration approval and eligibility will be verified by the Placement Team.

Is there any registration fee?

No. Registration and job application through the Udayan Care Placement Portal are completely free.

How can employers post jobs?

Employers can register their organization, complete the company profile and post verified job opportunities after approval.

How will I know if I'm shortlisted?

Once the backend is integrated, shortlisted candidates will receive notifications via email and through their dashboard.

Who should I contact for technical issues?

You can use the enquiry form above or email the Placement Team for any technical support regarding login, registration or profile issues.

Can employers search candidate profiles?

Yes. Approved employers will be able to search and view eligible candidate profiles based on job requirements.
